Getting Started

How Does The Process Work?


1. Email us a link to a website that you like (or your style preferences).

2. We create you a draft website using the example you sent us as a style guide.

3. You send us your content for each page.

4. You'll receive a temporary link to preview your website and request any revisions.

5. If you're happy with your design, you approve the website.

6. Invoice is sent through, we purchase your hosting & web address (or we can use your own). We'll email you your login details etc, and your website goes live!


It's easy and there are no obligations. Don't like our proposed design? Simply walk away!
